ABOUT ME

Growing up along the lakeshore in western Michigan, I developed a love of the outdoors, especially Lake Michigan. I love hiking through the woods and walking along the lakeshore, putting my feet in the warm sand and watching beautiful sunsets. I also love daisies, they hold a very special meaning for me. I have earned a Bachelors degree in Sociology and a Masters degree in Business. Recently, I started exploring web development and design. Since I am, at heart, a very analytical and logical thinker, web development and design has naturally become my passion. I am now in the process of making that passion more than just a dream.
